TaskFlow is not an ERP. There are no fee, inventory, or attendance modules. It's a lightweight chat + task system designed purely for accountability. Teachers learn it in five minutes.

Will teachers really use it?

Yes — because it's simpler than WhatsApp. They open a browser, see their tasks, and upload files. No app install, no password hassle. Schools report 95% adoption within a week.

What happens when a teacher delays a task?

The dashboard highlights delays automatically, and the scorecard reduces their on-time percentage. You get objective data for coaching and appraisals.

Yes. One-click export of task history, completion logs, and staff scorecards as CSV or PDF.
